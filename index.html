Join us as we walk you through our journey, achievements, and commitment to providing world-class comfort and efficiency.

INTRODUCTION

Established in 2019, we are a service-oriented company specializing in the design, installation, and maintenance of air conditioning and ventilation systems. Over the years, we have built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality solutions that meet the specific needs of our clients. We are proud to be an authorized distributor of Hisense and Gree products, and we have successfully installed VRF central air conditioning systems for multiple residential and commercial buildings, particularly in Pasay and Parañaque City, Philippines. Our team is highly skilled and experienced in handling the installation, commissioning, and maintenance of large-scale HVAC equipment from leading brands such as Daikin, Mitsubishi, Gree, and Hisense. Through our dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction, we continue to provide efficient and reliable solutions tailored to every project's unique requirements.

Our History

2019

The company's predecessor was VERDA Air Conditioning and Refrigeration Company, and became a distributor of the Gree brand.

2023

To meet the needs of company development, we established a new company to represent the new Hisense brand.

2024

We have an efficient and professional management team who have undergone training and are familiar with the installation, technical standards of VRF central air conditioning, leading to multi project completion and client satisfaction.

2025

As part of our growth strategy, we are expanding our expertise to a worldwide scale, aiming to provide innovative HVAC technologies and exceptional services across global markets.

Our Vision

To be a leading global HVAC solutions provider, recognized for our expertise in air conditioning, ventilation, and maintenance services.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class HVAC solutions through innovative design, professional installation, and reliable maintenance services, ensuring optimal comfort and efficiency for our clients.
